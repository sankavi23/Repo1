1.morning -  period of time between midnight and noon
2.evening -  period of time at the end of the day
3.night -  period from sunset to sunrise in each twenty-four hours
4.day - each of the twenty-four-hour periods
5.month - each of the twelve named periods into which a year is divided.
5.year - period of 365 or 366 days
6.second - Standard International ( SI ) unit of time.
7.minute - a period of time equal to sixty seconds 
8.hour - 60 minutes of time
9.past - occurred during a time previous to the present
10.present -  period of time that is happening now
12.future - the period of time that will come after the present time 
13.annual - occurring once every year.
14.century - a period of one hundred years.
15.calendar - a chart or series of pages showing the days, weeks, and months of a particular year.
16.everyday - happening or used every day
17.late - doing something or taking place after the expected
18.noon - twelve o'clock in the day
19.today - the present day
20.tomorrow - the day after today.
21.yesterday - on the day before today.
22.week - a period of seven days.
23.clock - an instrument for measuring and recording time
24.before -during the period of time preceding a particular event or time
25.after - later in time
